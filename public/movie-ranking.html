The Best Movies According to Alex

My top 3 movies of all-time.

1. The Lord of the Rings: The Return of the King

The third in line, but the best one out there.

2. The Lord of the Rings: The Two Towers

The second in line. This one is almost as good as the third one.

3. The Lord of the Rings: The Fellowship of the Ring

The first in line, but my least favorite of the three. But it's like choosing between one of your favorite children probably.
